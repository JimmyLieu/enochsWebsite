Enoch's Coffee & Winehouse

Launched in 2022, Enoch's Wind & Coffee House is located in the Southeast corner of Tyler, Texas. Our goal is to provide an upscale and enjoyable environment. for the community to come together and socialize. With ingredients sourced from all around the globe, we offer award-winning Enoch's wines as well as high-quality cofee, small plates, and pastries.
